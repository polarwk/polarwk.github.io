**Bitcoin Transaction De-anonymization:** This work builds upon previous network traffic analysis research to provide a comprehensive mechanism for tracking Bitcoin transactions. It utilizes probe nodes to collect traffic and analyzes this data to trace transaction input addresses, demonstrating effectiveness in distributed network environments.
